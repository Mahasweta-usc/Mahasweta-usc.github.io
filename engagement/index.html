    Workforce, Industrial and Societal Engagement

    The institute will accelerate the adoption of AI-based industry solutions in food systems, train the next generation food and agriculture workforce, and broaden community and public acceptance of AI technologies.

    Our Approach

    The Workforce, Industrial and Societal Engagement (WISEn) team will achieve this by:

    • Building both interdisciplinary networks of students and scientists across the U.S. as well as networks of industry, university and governments together to tackle critical food system challenges with AI-based solutions
    • Training graduate students, post-doctoral scholars and early career academics to engage society and industry on AI-related topics in food and agriculture
    • Creating internships for community college and 4-year undergraduate students on AI technologies to prepare for technology-related food and agriculture careers.
    • Partnering with the food, agriculture, technology and related industries to prioritize and accelerate product development.
    • Engaging the public on the beneficial uses of AI-based technologies and their impacts to communities, careers, food quality and the environment.

    First year engagement activities will include:

    • Weekly Seminar Series for AIFS institutions [link to page]
    • Science Says science communication training for institute scientists [link to page]
    • Internship program for community college and 4-year undergraduate students (coming soon)
    • AI for the Food System Virtual Conference and Workshops (coming soon)
    • An industry engagement program for industry-university collaboration, joint development, and technology commercialization (coming soon)
    • Educational AI and Machine learning materials, modules and bootcamps-specifically:
      • Incumbent Worker Level: Skill-set Competency Certificate
      • K-12 Level: Skill-set Competency Certificate
      • Community College Level: Skill-set Competency Certificate
      • University Level: Skill-set Competency Certificate
